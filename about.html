GET TO KNOW US

Hindu Jana Shakti – Nation First, Always! In-Service of Dharma.

Hindu Jana Shakti works tirelessly at the grassroots of Hindu Society, creating awareness and protecting our cultural heritage. We believe that a nation disconnected from its roots cannot flourish.

We Strongly Believe that a Nation which forgets its Roots will never stand for long. For this, we are working to safeguard our culture and tradition. In this, we also anticipate the support of all the Hindu Fraternity by PHYSICAL PARTICIPATION, TECHNICAL, INTELLECTUAL, LITERARY, SUPPORT, AND FINANCIAL SUPPORT.

Hindu Janashakthi is a socio-cultural movement committed to preserving, promoting, and protecting the timeless values of Sanatana Dharma while working for the holistic upliftment of society. Founded with the vision of awakening national consciousness and inspiring collective action, Hindu Janashakthi strives to build a self-reliant, spiritually aware, and socially responsible Bharat.

We believe that the power of the people — “Janashakti” — when rooted in dharma and unity, can bring about transformative change. Our mission is to foster cultural pride, serve communities in need, and nurture responsible citizens grounded in ethics, tradition, and service.

Our Core Objectives:

  • Protection of Hindu traditions is essential for national strength
  • Unity of Hindu society transcends all divisions
  • Service to society is service to Dharma
  • Active participation in safeguarding our culture is every Hindu’s responsibility
  • Cultural Revival: Promoting awareness and pride in Hindu heritage, rituals, festivals, and scriptures.
  • Social Welfare: Organizing health camps, education drives, environmental initiatives, and rural development programs.
  • Youth Empowerment: Engaging youth in leadership, volunteerism and spiritual education to shape future torchbearers of dharma.
  • National Integration: Uniting people beyond caste, region and language to strengthen the idea of one Bharat rooted in shared values.
  • Dharmic Awareness: Combating misinformation and upholding the true essence of Hindu philosophy, tolerance and universal harmony.

Vision

To create a spiritually awakened and socially empowered society rooted in dharma, patriotism and seva (selfless service).

Mission

To channel the strength of the people towards protecting Dharma, serving the nation and building a harmonious & united future.
