Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we manage the data you share with us when you visit our website, register interest, or make a donation.

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by Hindu Janajagruti Samiti, a non-profit organisation based in India. We provide information about our activities with an aim to involve like-minded organisations and individuals in our activities and accept donations to support our mission.

2. What Information Do We Collect?

2.1 Personal Information We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Registration Forms: Name, email address, phone number, address
  • Donations: Name, email, billing address, and payment details (via our payment processor)

We use this information to:

  • Contact you in response to your registration of interest
  • Explain our activities and provide further information upon request
  • Process and acknowledge donations

If you tell us you’re not interested, we will not contact you again unless you initiate further communication. 2.2 Non-Personal and Technical Information We automatically collect technical information to help us understand and improve our website, including:

2.2 Non-Personal and Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and usage statistics

This data is collected via cookies and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) and is not linked to personally identifiable information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We only use your personal information for the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • Responding to your inquiries or expression of interest
  • Sending updates (only if you've opted in)
  • Processing donations securely and issuing receipts

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, email services)
  • Authorities if required by law (e.g., for legal compliance)

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience and gather analytics. You may adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but some features of the site may not function properly.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL encryption
  • Secure third-party processors for donations
  • Access controls and periodic security reviews

7. Access and Control of Your Information

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at contact@hindujanashakti.org You can also opt out of communications using the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us directly.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact@hindujanashakti.org
