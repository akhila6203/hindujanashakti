Bharateeya Kanya Raksha

Standing Against Deception in the Name of Love

At Hindu Janashakthi, we believe in the sanctity of relationships built on trust, respect, and shared values. However, we have observed rising concerns from various communities about instances where religious identity is deliberately concealed to exploit emotional bonds for the purpose of religious conversion. This deceptive practice, often referred to as "Bharateeya Kanya Raksha," threatens the harmony and trust between communities.

We stand firmly against any form of deceit or coercion in the name of love. Every individual has the right to make informed choices about their relationships, and those choices should be free from manipulation or hidden agendas.

Our Objectives:

  • Awareness Campaigns: Educating families and youth about their rights, safe relationships, and recognizing red flags of manipulation.
  • Legal Support: Providing guidance and legal assistance to victims and their families.
  • Community Engagement: Encouraging open dialogue and preventive measures within the community.
  • Empowering Women: Supporting women with education, self-defense training, and awareness of their rights under the law.

We are not against inter-faith relationships based on mutual respect and truth. But we firmly oppose any relationship rooted in deception, coercion, or radical agendas.

A Call for Vigilance and Unity

Our goal is to protect the dignity and freedom of every individual, while preserving the cultural and spiritual fabric of our society. Together, let us build a future where relationships are genuine, respectful, and empowering.
