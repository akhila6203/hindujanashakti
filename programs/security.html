Social Utility

Brahmin Seva Swayampakam (TG Covid Crisis Hindu Responsibility): During the 2020 Covid crisis, Hindu Jana Shakti supported temple priests and the Archakar community by providing food supplies and rations to 1,000 Brahmin families through the Swayampakam initiative.

We aim to protect and promote the environment by addressing climate change, managing municipal and solid waste, and encouraging recycling activities. Our efforts also include disaster mitigation and management, drought relief, promoting sustainable agricultural systems, zero-based natural farming, plantations, social forestry, pollution control awareness, and eco-tourism to safeguard our natural heritage.

We work towards the empowerment of women by organizing them into self-help groups and providing capacity-building training. This initiative helps them fight against domestic violence and atrocities, ensuring their legitimate rights across all age groups are protected.
