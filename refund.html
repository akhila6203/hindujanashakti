Refund and Cancellation Policy

At Hindu Jana Shaki Foundation, we are deeply grateful for the generosity and support of our donors. Every contribution helps us further our mission and create meaningful impact. While donations are generally considered final and non-refundable, we understand that certain circumstances may require exceptions. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which a donor may request a refund.

1. Failed or Unsuccessful Transactions

  • If a donation is not successfully processed due to technical issues, banking errors, or unavoidable circumstances beyond the donor’s control, the donation will be automatically cancelled.
  • The donor will receive a full refund of the deducted amount within 3–4 working days, depending on the payment method and banking channels involved.

2. Improper Utilisation of Funds

  • We are committed to transparency and responsible use of donated funds.
  • In the unlikely event that a donor, after a proper inquiry by the concerned authority, is not satisfied with the manner in which the funds have been utilised by the organisation, the donor may raise a refund request.
  • Upon review and approval, the donated amount shall be refunded and credited to the original payment method within 3–4 working days.

3. Refund Request Procedure

  • All refund requests must be submitted in writing to hindujanashakti@gmail.com, clearly stating the reason for the request along with proof of donation (such as transaction ID, receipt, or confirmation email).
  • Our team will verify the details and process eligible refunds in accordance with this policy.

4. Exclusions

  • Donations made voluntarily and successfully processed without technical errors are typically non-refundable.
  • Refunds will not be issued if funds have already been utilised for the purpose they were collected, unless covered under the exceptions mentioned above.

5. Processing Time

  • Once approved, refunds will be credited to the donor’s original mode of payment within 3–4 working days, subject to the policies of the donor’s bank or payment service provider.

6. Governing Law

This Refund Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising in connection with this policy shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts located in Telangana.
