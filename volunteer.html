Volunteer

  • Our mission is to educate the masses about the greatness of our culture and dharma.
  • We also aim to raise awareness about misleading practices, scams, and unethical activities occurring in the name of religion or faith.
  • We respect people of all faiths and religious backgrounds.
  • Our work is not intended to hurt, criticize, or challenge anyone’s religious beliefs.
  • Volunteers must maintain a respectful and inclusive approach in all activities.
  • Volunteers must treat everyone with kindness, avoiding discrimination or bias.
  • If you would like to join our mission in the promotion, propagation and protection of Sanathana Dharma, please fill the form below and we will reach out to you shortly.
